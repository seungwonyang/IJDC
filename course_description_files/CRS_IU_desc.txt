DIGITAL CURATION 

Upon successful completion of this course, students should be able to: 
• Demonstrate  knowledge  of  research,  policy  and  current  practice  in  curating  and  preserving  digital data; 
• Reflect on researchers’ and practitioners’ realQworld experiences in performing digital curation; 
• Demonstrate  knowledge  of  the  Digital  Curation  Centre  (DCC)  Curation  Lifecycle  Model  and  be 
able to apply it as a framework for understanding digital curation in various contexts; and 
become familiar with digital curation computing environments and curating digital materials based on hands-on experience
