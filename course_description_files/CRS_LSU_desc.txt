Introduction to Digital Curation (Fall 2016)

Digital curation can be thought of as an entire process of management and care for digital content. Ensuring a long-term preservation, access, and reuse of digital content is also a major concern. In this introductory course, we will explore various aspects of digital and data curation including its definition, lifecycle, relevant technologies, data repositories, (web) archiving, preservation, and so on. Students will be engaged in a term project (teams), homework assignments (individually), and activities (individually).

Discuss definitions and perspectives of digital and data curation.
Explore various technologies which form the technical basis of digital and data curation. Acquire hands-on experience for digital and data curation.
